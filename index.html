How it works

Three taps.
Parked.

Step 01

Find a spot

Open the map and see every on-street bay colour-coded by status. Green means free right now. Red means paid. Filter by what you need.

Step 02

Check the details

Tap any spot to see its operating hours, hourly rate, bay count, and live crowd reports from other drivers. Then hit navigate or park here.

Step 03

Park and relax

One tap marks your car. A timer starts and we'll notify you before time runs out. When you're done, we'll walk you straight back.

Account management

Delete your sloto. account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. This action is irreversible — all your data will be removed from our servers.

⚠️
This cannot be undone. Deleting your account permanently removes your profile, all crowd reports you've submitted, spot reports, feedback, and your earned badges. Your locally-saved parked car state will remain on your device until you uninstall the app.
Recommended

Delete directly in the app

1
Open sloto. and go to the Profile tab
Tap the person icon in the bottom navigation bar to open your Profile screen.
2
Make sure you're signed in
Account deletion requires you to be signed in. If you're not signed in, tap "Continue with Google" first.
3
Scroll down and tap "Delete account"
The delete option appears in red text at the bottom of the Profile screen, below the Account section.
4
Confirm in the first dialog
A confirmation dialog will appear explaining that this action is permanent. Tap "Delete" to proceed to the second step.
5
Type DELETE to confirm
A second confirmation asks you to type exactly:
DELETE
This two-step process prevents accidental deletion. Once confirmed, your account and all data are permanently removed.
or
Via email

Request deletion by email

1
Email us at hello@sloto.ie
Send an email from the address associated with your sloto. account with the subject line "Account deletion request".
2
Include your account email address
Confirm the email address you used to sign in to sloto. so we can locate and delete the correct account.
3
We'll process your request within 7 days
We'll send you a confirmation once your account and all associated data have been permanently deleted from our servers.
What gets permanently deleted
Your user profile (name, email, avatar)
All crowd reports you submitted
All spot reports you submitted
All wrong info reports you submitted
Any feedback messages you sent
Your spots reported count and badges
Any photos you uploaded with reports
Your Google OAuth connection
Questions about data deletion or your rights under GDPR? Contact us at hello@sloto.ie. We respond within 24 hours and are required to action deletion requests within 30 days under GDPR.

Account management

Delete your account

Permanently remove your sloto. account and all associated data. This cannot be undone.

⚠️
This is permanent. Deleting your account removes your profile, all reports you've submitted, feedback, and your badges. Your locally-saved parking state will remain on your device until you uninstall the app.
Recommended

Delete in the app

1
Open sloto. → Profile tab
Tap the person icon in the bottom nav.
2
Make sure you're signed in
Tap "Continue with Google" if you're not already.
3
Tap "Delete account" (red, bottom of screen)
Appears below the Account section.
4
Confirm in the first dialog
Tap "Delete" to proceed.
5
Type DELETE to confirm
A second step prevents accidental deletion. Type exactly:
DELETE
or
Via email

Request by email

1
Email hello@sloto.ie
Send from the address associated with your account. Subject: "Account deletion request".
2
Include your account email
So we can locate and delete the correct account.
3
We'll process within 7 days
You'll receive a confirmation once deletion is complete.
What gets permanently deleted
Your profile (name, email, avatar)
All crowd reports you submitted
All spot reports you submitted
All wrong info reports
Feedback messages
Your badges and spots reported count
Uploaded photos
Google OAuth connection
Questions about data deletion or GDPR rights? Email hello@sloto.ie. We respond within 24 hours and action deletion requests within 30 days as required by GDPR.

Our edge

AI is the product, not a feature

Most parking apps show you a map and make you figure it out. Sloto lets you ask a question and get an answer. That's a fundamentally different experience — and a much stickier one.

🤖
Natural language search
Drivers type queries like "free parking near the Aviva" or "cheapest spot near Grafton Street" and get 3 ranked results instantly — with walking time, cost analysis, and AI reasoning. Powered by Claude Haiku via a Vercel serverless function. 10 searches per day on the free tier.
🌏
1,000+ spots mapped
Every on-street parking spot across Dublin city, south Dublin, and north Dublin. Zone colour, hourly rate, operating hours, bay count — all bundled at build time. No expensive backend.
👥
Live crowd reports
Drivers report availability in real time — Plenty, A Few, or Full. The AI factors this into its recommendations. Reports expire after 60 minutes, keeping data fresh.
The opportunity

A city of drivers with nowhere to park

Dublin has one of the highest car ownership rates in Europe and a chronic shortage of affordable parking. Drivers waste time and money every single day — and no one has solved it with AI yet.

  • Over 1.4 million cars registered in the Greater Dublin Area
  • Drivers waste significant time on every trip searching for parking
  • Hundreds of thousands of parking fines issued annually - many avoidable
  • No AI-powered parking discovery app exists in the Irish market
  • Sloto already maps 1,000+ spots with AI search — a genuine technical head start

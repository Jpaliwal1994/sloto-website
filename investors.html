The opportunity

Dublin parking is genuinely broken.

1.4 million cars in Greater Dublin. Minutes wasted searching on every trip. €80+ fines. And zero purpose-built apps for Irish on-street parking. Until now.

The problem
Drivers are flying blind

Dublin drivers circle streets with no idea what's paid, what's free, or when restrictions change. Every trip is a guess.

  • No single source of truth for on-street Dublin parking
  • Free vs paid depends on day, time, and zone — impossible to memorise
  • €80+ average fine when you get it wrong
  • Existing apps (Google Maps, Apple Maps) show car parks, not street bays
The solution
sloto. knows the answer

We've mapped every verified on-street bay and compute free vs paid status in real time from council operating hours data.

  • 1,279 spots with correct zone, rate, and operating hours
  • Status computed on-device — works instantly, no server round trip
  • Crowd reports layer real-time availability on top
  • Free Sundays and bank holidays handled automatically

The opportunity

A city of drivers with nowhere to park

Dublin has one of the highest car ownership rates in Europe and a chronic shortage of affordable parking. Drivers waste time and money every single day.

  • Over 1.4 million cars registered in the Greater Dublin Area
  • Drivers waste significant time on every trip searching for parking
  • Hundreds of thousands of parking fines issued annually - many avoidable
  • No dominant real-time parking discovery app exists in the Irish market
  • A scalable, low-cost data model makes national expansion highly viable

Business model

Free to grow. Built to monetise.

We're focused on building a loyal user base first. Monetisation options are clear and proven - we'll layer them in once we have traction.

Now
Free
The app is free to download and use. The priority right now is getting Dublin drivers on board and building a habit around Sloto.
Near-term
Subscriptions
A premium tier for power users - commuters, delivery drivers, field teams. Recurring revenue with low churn: once someone stops paying for parking, they stay.
Long-term
Expand
Cork, Galway, Limerick and beyond. The same model works in every Irish city, and the playbook is already proven in Dublin.

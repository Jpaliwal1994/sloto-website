The opportunity

Dublin parking is
genuinely broken.

1.4 million cars. Minutes wasted per trip. €80+ fines. Zero purpose-built apps for Irish on-street parking.

The problem
Drivers are flying blind
  • Free vs paid depends on day, time, and zone — impossible to memorise. Existing apps show car parks, not street bays.
  • No single source of truth for on-street Dublin parking
  • €80+ average fine when you get it wrong
  • Google Maps and Apple Maps don't cover street bays
The solution
sloto. knows the answer
  • 1,200+ spots with correct zone, rate, and operating hours. Status computed on-device, instantly.
  • Free vs paid computed from council data in real time
  • Crowd reports layer live availability on top
  • Free Sundays and bank holidays handled automatically
